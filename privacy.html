MindWell Solution Group LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates The Angle application and website (theangle.ai).

Information We Collect

The Angle stores your preferences (name, business category) locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers.

Payment Information

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details. Please review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party advertising. Your usage data stays on your device.

Data Storage

All app data (progress, streaks, XP) is stored locally in your browser. Clearing your browser data will reset your progress. We have no access to this data.

Children's Privacy

The Angle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
